Diagnostic Tests

Simple Process

From Booking to Report in 24 Hours

1

Book a Home Collection Online

Use our 3-step booking wizard to select your pet's test, choose your city and preferred time slot, and enter your contact details. Takes under 2 minutes. Payment can be made online or cash on collection.

2

Certified Agent Arrives at Your Home

Our trained phlebotomist will WhatsApp you 30 minutes before arrival. They carry sterile, single-use equipment and are trained in calm, fear-free animal handling. The process takes 5–10 minutes and your pet stays in their safe environment.

3

Sample Reaches the Lab

The sample is transported in temperature-controlled containers to our diagnostic partner lab within 2 hours. Chain of custody is documented at every stage. You'll receive an SMS once the sample is received and processing begins.

4

Digital Report with Vet Summary

Your report is delivered as a downloadable PDF within 24 hours, along with a plain-language interpretation written by a licensed vet. No confusing medical jargon — just clear guidance on what the results mean and what to do next.
